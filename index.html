I am a Solution Architect at xMatters focused on helping customers implementing DevOps and SRE concepts connect isolated systems and automate processes and user touch points.

Previous to xMatters I spent 8 years at a large hardware corporation going through digital transformation. During this time I held roles as Product Manager as well as managing SREs and operations within a development organization implementing DevOps concepts. This included transformations of their platform from on-prem to cloud, converting to containers, and implementing microservices. I worked on a range of projects building platforms and services to help customers improve their customer experience through digital operations and interaction.

xMatters Integration Projects

These are xMatters integrations I have worked on.

Azure DevOps Boards

Allows you to send new work item alerts to xMatters so that you can send notifications or trigger customized workflows. It also includes steps to create and update a work item as well as add comments to a work item.

  • Check It Out

Azure DevOps Pipelines

Allows you to trigger an ADO Pipeline from a xMatters workflow. Also gives the ability to send notifications to xMatters from a pipeline task or release gate.

  • Check It Out

Jira

Various Flow Designer steps that can be used with Jira and the builtin triggers and steps.

  • Check It Out

Salesforce - Case Alert

This is a one-way integration with Salesforce that will trigger an xMatters flow when a case is created. It can be used along with the builtin xMatters Salesforce steps to create a closed-loop integration.

  • Check It Out
